Clarified the role of the pre-concept phase as a **strategic innovation stage preceding advanced design**, where designers can generate future-oriented ideas grounded in cultural insight, storytelling, and techno-imagination.

- Outlined a systematic pre-concept methodology — including perception, observation, insight extraction, solution exploration, and evaluation — supported by automotive interior case studies.
- Highlighted how rapid prototyping, digital simulation (VPD/VR/AR), and concept-tree mapping enhance feasibility assessment and elevate product competitiveness.
- Positioned pre-concept design as a corporate innovation mechanism that links long-term product direction, cross-functional coordination, and early-stage roadmap building.
