Introduced a front-end **"Pre-Concept" methodology** that integrates scenario planning into early automotive innovation, enabling concept generation before formal product planning begins.

- Illustrated how long-range scenario insights can be shaped into actionable concept pathways, supporting leadership in connecting long-term vision with short-term development priorities.
- Established a communication structure that aligns innovation-oriented and execution-driven leadership approaches, strengthening organizational acceptance of future-oriented concepts.
- Confirmed the methodology's applicability through industry case applications and leadership feedback, positioning it as a practical tool for early-stage concept alignment.
