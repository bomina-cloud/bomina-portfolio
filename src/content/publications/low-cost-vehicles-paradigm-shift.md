Analyzed future market paradigm shifts and emerging markets to identify the strategic need for **low-cost vehicles driven by new consumer value trends**.

- Proposed design methodologies for cost-efficient yet emotionally appealing vehicles using simplification, essential-function design, modularity, and material reduction.
- Demonstrated how innovative **"minus-design"** and structural optimization can create sustainable, affordable vehicle models suitable for expanding Asian markets.
