Master's thesis. Identified major shifts in emerging automotive markets and the growing needs of Bottom-of-Pyramid (BOP) consumers, establishing the strategic demand for value-driven low-cost vehicles.

- Introduced key design principles — **simplicity, flexibility, extended meaning, and human-centered usability** — to create affordable yet emotionally resonant automotive concepts.
- Validated how cost-efficient structuring, modularity, and optimized packaging can deliver low-cost vehicles without sacrificing product quality or consumer experience.
